About Alefoods

At Alefoods, we bring the authentic taste of Peru to the world. We specialize in exporting high-quality, natural foods, including our select coffee from Oxapampa and ready-to-eat quinoa from the Andes. Our mission is to deliver exceptional flavor and nutrition while upholding the highest standards of quality and social responsibility.
